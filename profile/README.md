# 📚&nbsp;&nbsp;Learn Together Lowcountry *(LTL)*

**👋 &nbsp;&nbsp;Welcome to the GitHub organization for the Learn Together Lowcountry Homeschool Cooperative!**

## 🤷&nbsp;&nbsp;WHAT IS LTL?

We offer co-op classes on Fridays, core academic classes on Thursdays *(LTL Plus)*, special events, tutoring, and support through Legacies Homeschool Association. We serve over 250 co-op students (ages 0-19), 120+ families, and 1,500+ annual participants through offering connection, instruction, and support.

## 📅 &nbsp; WHEN IS LTL?

Our co-op and core classes meet from August to May. Friday co-op runs for 24 weeks, and LTL Plus classes run for 34 weeks. Events, field trips, and gatherings happen year-round to give families consistent support and connection across every season.

##  🤔 &nbsp; WHY LTL?

Because **homeschooling is better together**. We’re building a connected, thriving homeschool community that supports academic growth, friendships, faith, and fun — helping families succeed in their homeschool journey from nursery through high school.
